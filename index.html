Analysis of Chase Bank deposits data in United States

In this Assignment, Chase Banks deposits data(2010-2016) has been used to visualize the deposits amount made by customers in United States. Chase is currently present in 26 states in United States. The below GeoMap provides an analysis of the deposited amount per state in USA. When hovered over the state it provides the total amount that is deposited by customers in that particular state over the span of 7 years(2010-2017). When clicked on the particular state it displays a second graph representing year wise deposited data.

  • Chloropleth-raw
  • Chloropleth-Aggregated
  • DotMap

From the visualizations presented above it can be observed that New York and Ohio states generated a deposits greater than billion dollars where as states such as Georgia and Idaho has generated deposits less that 10 million. From the GeoMap it can also be observed that Chase still has to establish its branches in many states in United States.
